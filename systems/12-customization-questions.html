Customization Questions System

Interactive onboarding and configuration that unlocks Spaces, roles, jobs, and access as a guided experience.

Overview

This system replaces static onboarding with an interactive sequence of questions that map a user’s choices to unlock rules, role assignments, and customized Space visibility. It makes onboarding cinematic, deterministic, and data-driven.

  • Interactive unlock flows
  • Role & job assignment
  • Guided onboarding widgets

How it fits in Nexus OS

Customization Questions plug into City OS and Core to provision access and tailor the initial user experience while emitting events to Automation and Analytics.
