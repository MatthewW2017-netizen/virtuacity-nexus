Automation Engine

Automations to auto-assign jobs, unlock Spaces, run onboarding, promote, reward, and log events.

Overview

The Automation Engine empowers Cities and Studios to declare rules and triggers that run reliably: from automated job assignments in RP to dynamic unlocks for Spaces, and scheduled rewards. Automations are auditable and can be simulated before activation.

  • Trigger-action workflows
  • Auditable automation runs
  • Simulations & safe deployments

How it fits in Nexus OS

Automation reduces manual overhead for Owners and Teams while enabling consistent, repeatable behaviors across RP, Studio, Creator, and Mini‑App systems.
