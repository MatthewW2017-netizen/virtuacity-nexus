Social Graph

A network of collaboration based on shared RP, Studio, Creator, and Mini‑App activity—not simple friends or followers.

Overview

The Social Graph maps meaningful connections derived from collaborations, co-participation in events, project histories, and Mini‑App interactions. It surfaces relevant collaborations and suggests teams, creators, and resources based on real activity patterns.

  • Activity-driven relationships
  • Collaboration discovery
  • Privacy-aware graph exposure

How it fits in Nexus OS

Social Graph powers discovery, Creator matchmaking, Studio hiring, and RP team formation—without reverting to simplistic friend/follower models.
