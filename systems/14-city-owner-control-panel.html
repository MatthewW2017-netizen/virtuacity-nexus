City Owner Control Panel

The Owner OS: create/link Spaces, set unlock rules, identity rules, visibility, age and membership rules, and attach Mini‑Apps/Bots/RP/Studio/Creator systems.

Overview

The City Owner Control Panel is a powerful administrative surface that allows owners to define governance, visibility, and attachments for their City. Owners can define unlock trees, moderation rules, age gating, membership policies, and deploy Mini‑Apps or bots into Spaces safely.

  • Governance & unlock rules
  • Identity & visibility controls
  • Attach and configure Mini‑Apps/Bots

How it fits in Nexus OS

Owner decisions here propagate through Security, Automation, Theme, and Analytics to ensure the City behaves cohesively and safely.
