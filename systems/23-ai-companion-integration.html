AI Companion Integration

Mode-specific copilots for RP, Studio, Creator, Bot Dev, Mini‑App, and City Owner assistance.

Overview

AI Companions are contextual copilots tailored to Modes: RP AI helps worldbuilding and logs, Studio AI assists code and QA, Creator AI helps portfolios, Bot Dev AI suggests diagnostics, and City Owner AI provides governance suggestions. All companions operate under transparency and consent controls.

  • Mode-specific copilots
  • Transparent suggestions & logs
  • Permissioned access & human oversight

How it fits in Nexus OS

AI Companions augment human workflows across Nexus while integrating with Analytics, Automation, and Security for safe, auditable assistance.
