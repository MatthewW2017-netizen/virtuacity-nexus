Presence Engine

Real-time signals showing which Space, Mode, Mini‑App, RP job, or Studio task a user is engaged with.

Overview

The Presence Engine aggregates and exposes user state across the OS, enabling live awareness, contextual actions, and dynamic discovery. It also powers the Member Panel and streaming/voice indicators for a coherent view of activity.

  • Real-time activity aggregation
  • Contextual discovery and presence badges
  • Feeds for analytics and automation

How it fits in Nexus OS

Presence signals inform Automation, Analytics, and the Member Panel—helping Cities and Creators understand engagement and drive timely interactions.
