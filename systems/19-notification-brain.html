Notification Brain

Smart, mode-aware notifications that prioritize RP calls, Studio builds, and Creator events while deferring low-priority alerts.

Overview

The Notification Brain is an intent-driven prioritization layer: it understands Mode context, membership levels, and user focus to surface what matters now and silence what can wait. It integrates with Automation to schedule and batch messages.

  • Priority & context-aware alerts
  • Delay, batching, and digest modes
  • Integration with Automation and Presence

How it fits in Nexus OS

Notifications connect user attention to critical OS events and drive engagement for Owners, Creators, and Studios while reducing noise and respecting privacy.
