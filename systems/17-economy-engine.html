Economy Engine

Global and City-specific currencies, shops, banks, creator marketplace, Mini‑App micro-economies, and membership rewards.

Overview

The Economy Engine supports multiple currencies, payment rails, shops, and banking primitives for Cities, Creators, and Mini‑Apps. It enables boosts, membership bonuses, creator payouts, and microtransactions while integrating with Analytics and Security for fraud prevention.

  • Multi-currency & wallets
  • Shops, banks, and marketplaces
  • Membership rewards and boosts

How it fits in Nexus OS

Economy is a cross-cutting concern: it powers Creator monetization, RP banks, Mini‑App tokens, and boost systems for Cities.
