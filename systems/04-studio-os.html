Studio OS

A development suite at OS level — teams, logs, QA, asset pipelines, updates, and Studio Guide.

Overview

Studio OS provides a production-grade environment for building, testing, and deploying Mini‑Apps, City features, and Studio-specific workflows. It includes role-based teams, changelogs, QA panels, asset management, and secure deployment pipelines that integrate with Nexus Core identity and ownership controls.

  • Team and role management
  • Logs, QA boards, and asset registry
  • Studio Guide and release workflows

How it fits in Nexus OS

Studio OS is the toolset for creators and teams to ship content into Cities and Mini-Apps, tied into Automation, Analytics, and the Theme Engine for coherent releases.
