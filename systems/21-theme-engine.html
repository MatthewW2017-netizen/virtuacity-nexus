Theme Engine

City-level theming: colors, fonts, animations, Space layouts, badge styles, and skins for Mini‑Apps.

Overview

The Theme Engine gives City Owners and Studio Leads control over visual identity: color palettes, typography, motion preferences, Space layouts, and asset skins — all applied consistently across the OS while allowing graceful overrides.

  • Palettes and typography
  • Animation and motion controls
  • Skinning for Mini‑Apps and badges

How it fits in Nexus OS

Themes are applied at the City level and cascade into Profiles, Member Panels, and Mini‑Apps—ensuring every City feels distinct while staying within performance budgets.
