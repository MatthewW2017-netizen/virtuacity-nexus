Analytics OS

Insights for City Owners, Studio Leads, and Creators — activity, engagement, productivity, and Mini‑App usage.

Overview

Analytics OS aggregates telemetry and events into actionable dashboards: RP engagement, Studio velocity, Creator conversions, Mini‑App retention, and membership metrics. Data is permissioned, anonymized where appropriate, and surfaced to Owners with clear PII controls.

  • Ownership & privacy-aware analytics
  • Dashboards for Cities and Creators
  • Event-driven reports and alerts

How it fits in Nexus OS

Analytics pulls from Presence, Chat, Economy, and Automation to provide owners and creators with the intelligence to make informed decisions.
