Creator OS

A creator-centric layer: tiers, badges, portfolios, asset stores, requests, and Creator Guide.

Overview

Creator OS empowers individual creators and small studios with tools for publishing portfolios, managing tiers, issuing badges, processing requests, and monetizing assets across Cities. It includes discovery, reviews, and a Creator Guide to help creators scale their work within Nexus.

  • Portfolios, tiers, and badges
  • Asset publishing & reviews
  • Creator Guide and discovery feeds

How it fits in Nexus OS

Creator OS links creators to Cities and Studios, integrates with Economy and Inventory systems, and surfaces Creator analytics to help grow sustainable creator businesses.
