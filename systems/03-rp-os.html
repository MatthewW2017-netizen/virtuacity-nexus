RP OS

The roleplay engine: jobs, units, dispatch, logs, fines, auras, power-ups, RP bank and RP shop.

Overview

RP OS provides deterministic roleplay systems: assignable jobs, dispatch mechanics, unit management, event logs, and in-world economies. It's designed to support structured gameplay and emergent storytelling, while integrating with Identity and Presence for immersive experiences.

  • Job & unit definitions
  • Dispatch & call systems
  • RP economy, fines, and power-ups

How it fits in Nexus OS

RP OS consumes Presence, Inventory, and Economy modules to orchestrate narrative systems, while emitting events to Analytics and Automation for owner insights and automated behaviors.
