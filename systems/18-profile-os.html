Profile OS

Full identity cards — RP, Studio, Creator identities; badges, auras, power-ups, Spaces unlocked, and Mini‑Apps installed.

Overview

Profile OS is the authoritative identity layer: it composes personas across Modes and Cities while preserving provenance, badges, and activity history. Profiles are extensible and respect privacy and verification from the Core and Security subsystems.

  • Composable personas
  • Badges, auras, and power-ups
  • Privacy & verification controls

How it fits in Nexus OS

Profiles are referenced by Chat, RP, Analytics, and the Economy—acting as the single source of truth for a user's across-Nexus identity and entitlements.
