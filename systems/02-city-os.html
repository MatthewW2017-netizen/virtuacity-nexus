City OS

A city is a living instance of Nexus — onboarding, Spaces, boosts, identity controls, and Owner tools.

Overview

City OS encapsulates everything a City Owner needs to run a digital city: Space creation and linking, onboarding flows, boost mechanics, identity rules, and analytics. Each City is themeable and can operate under its own guide while remaining part of the global Nexus fabric.

  • Spaces lifecycle and linking
  • Boosts & membership tiers
  • City Owner control surface and analytics

How it fits in Nexus OS

City OS composes Core profiles and Expansion modules to create a tailored city experience with its own rules, themes, and governance—while integrating with automation, economy, and presence engines for a cohesive experience.
