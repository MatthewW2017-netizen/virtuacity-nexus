Bot Developer OS

A platform for building safe, sandboxed bots: commands, diagnostics, panels, permissions, and Bot Guide.

Overview

Bot Developer OS gives developers a secure, sandboxed environment to author bots that interact with Nexus systems: command registries, diagnostics, structured logs, and permission grants. The platform emphasizes safety, clear permissions, and inspectable behavior for owners and users.

  • Command & panel registration
  • Diagnostics, logs, and sandboxing
  • Bot Guide and permission model

How it fits in Nexus OS

Bots extend every Mode: they can power Mini‑Apps, enhance Studio workflows, assist Creators, and respond to RP events—while Security OS enforces isolation and audit trails.
