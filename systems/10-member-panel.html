Member Panel

A right-side OS panel: search, Spaces, RP units, Studio teams, Creators, Bots, Mini‑Apps, and streaming indicators.

Overview

The Member Panel is the persistent right-hand OS surface: it shows who is online, active Spaces, RP unit statuses, Team presence, installed Mini‑Apps, and streaming/voice indicators. It acts as a control center for personal context and quick actions.

  • Search & quick actions
  • Live presence and unit states
  • App & stream shortcuts

How it fits in Nexus OS

The Member Panel surfaces cross-system information and acts as a launching point for Spaces, Mini‑Apps, and collaborative Studio workflows while respecting privacy and membership rules from Security OS.
