Mini-App OS

A lightweight launcher and runtime for small apps — triggers, permissions, publishing, and runtime integrations.

Overview

Mini‑App OS provides a constrained runtime for small interactive experiences: event triggers, permission scopes, publishing workflows, and deep integration points with RP, Studio, Creator, and Bot systems. Mini‑Apps are discoverable within Cities and can be attached to Spaces or Modes.

  • Trigger & permission model
  • Publish, version, and integrate
  • Runtime events and cross-system hooks

How it fits in Nexus OS

Mini‑Apps are first-class citizens in Nexus: they extend Space behaviors, tie into Automation, and can be surfaced by the Presence Engine when active.
