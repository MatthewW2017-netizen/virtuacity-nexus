  2. Submit appeal within 30 days with new evidence or context
  3. Review by a different mod team member (appeals are never reviewed by original actioner)
  4. Decision within 14 days with full explanation
  5. Escalation available for serious cases to our Appeals Board

â“Frequently Asked Questions

How long do appeals take to review?

We aim to review all appeals within 14 days. Complex cases may take longer, and we'll keep you updated.

Can I appeal multiple times?

Each action can be appealed once. If new evidence emerges, you can submit a new appeal with that evidence.

What if I disagree with the appeal decision?

Serious cases can be escalated to our Appeals Board. You'll have the opportunity to request escalation in the appeal decision.

Is my report information confidential?

Yes. Reports are confidential and only reviewed by our moderation team. We only share your identity if required by law.

What happens if I receive a permanent ban?

Permanent bans are only issued for serious violations. You can still appeal, but the appeals process is more rigorous.
