What is Nexus OS?

Nexus is a living operating system for digital cities and worlds — website-first, app-second. It combines Social, RP, Studio, Creator, Bot, and Mini‑App systems into a single OS-level experience.

  • Website-first: full OS in the browser
  • App-second: desktop & mobile apps powered by the same OS
  • OS concepts: Spaces, Modes, Panels, Guides, and Owner Controls
